FIREARMS

Items:

Ingredients

Improvised Rifle

Make a wooden stock and add a pipe to it, find around 10 pieces of cable coil and add it, then screwdriver it - add some plasteel, wrench that and finally some more metal. Screwdriver it and add another pipe and some metal rods - and finally weld it. It fires 7.62, which you could obtain from a autolathe likely, or steal from weapons.

Crossbow

You’ll need 5 wooden planks to make the frame of this, and then add 3 metal rods to it. Weld the frame, add 5 pieces of cable, a small amount of plastic, another 5 pieces of cable and screwdriver it. It uses steel rods as ammo.

Zipgun

You’ll need 5 wooden planks to create the stock. Then, add a straight pipe, some duct-tape to hold it together, a mouse-trap and finally screwdriver it. Make sure you examine what rounds it fires, as they vary on completion.

Coil Gun

Get 5 wood to make a stock, add 5 sheets of metal, add some duct-tape, a pipe, then a welding tool and 5 cables. Then, finally three SMES coils, and screwdriver it to finish.

Flamethrower

Find yourself a welding tool, use a screwdriver on it to make it attachable. Attach some metal rods, and then find a igniter (usually in tool storage, or engineering), screwdriver it and attach it to the welding tool. Ask, or steal a plasma tank - Look in science, engineering, or EVA, or wrench it out of a smaller vessel. Attach it and get cooking.

Pneumatic Cannon

Get some metal sheets and create a cannon shaft, then add a pipe to it and weld it on. Add some additional metal and weld it again, then you’ll need to add a tank transfer valve - You can’t usually find these on the Nerva, but you could find them on ships, or through other contacts. Weld it again to finish it. It may require an air tank for fuel.

MELEE WEAPONS

Items:

Ingredients

Baseball Bat

You’ll need to find some metal to craft a bat, around 10 sheets.

Stun Prod

Obtain some metal rods, make a spear shaft and attach wirecutters to the shaft. Then, add a battery (which can easily be produced in a protolathe) and start stunning those Shitsecs.

Glass Shard Spear

Obtain some metal and make some steel rods, some cable coils to tie it together and some glass sharps. Create cable restraints first, and then apply them onto your metal rod and finally affix a glass shard on top for some real damage.

Metal Spear

Find some metal rods again, and some cable coil for restraints, apply the coil onto the rods to make a shaft. Next you’ll need to make a knife blade from some metal (around 6 sheets) and then apply the blade to the spear shaft.

Makeshift Shield

Procure some plasteel and start crafting, this will protect you from some melee damage, but be aware of stuns!

Floor Shuriken

Wrench up some floor tiles with a crowbar, or find some nearby and sharpen the edges of them with a wirecutter to increase the damage they do, fling directly at the Captain.

Butterfly Knife

Find some sort of metal to use, and create a blade from it. Then, create a knife hilt and screwdriver it, allowing you to attach the blade. Remember that you can conceal these blades in boots.

ARMOUR

Hazard Vest Armour

Cut some holes into a hazard vest, commonly found in maintenance. Then, add some wires to the vest, and a metal sheet, and finally finish with more wires. This offers some moderate protection.

Cardboard AI Disguise

Wearing both a cardboard helmet, and a cardboard suit will trick the AI into thinking you're a cyborg on its sensors, use this to your advantage!

IRON-WORKING

Items:

Ingredients

ANVIL

Requires 12 Iron Ingot found likely from mining. You’ll need a blacksmithing hammer, which can easily be made in a autolathe in Cargo.

SABRE

Create at the Anvil.

LONG SWORD

Create at the Anvil.

RAPIER

Create at the Anvil.

HUNTING KNIFE

Create at the Anvil.

MUSKET

Create gun parts and a musket barrel at the anvil, then use wood to create the rifle stock - From there, you just combine them together. It requires musket balls for ammo, and you must open and close the gun to reload it.

PICKAXE

Craft a pickaxe head and add some wooden rods to create a pickaxe.

AXE

Craft a axe head and then add some wooden rods to create an axe.
